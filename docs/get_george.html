Testing  

George-TEST is for anyone who wants to help test new features and new versions before they are deployed to George"(production). Don't rely on George-TEST for regular use, as it may change unexpectedly and sometimes even break.

George-TEST installs and runs as a completely separate application from George. You may therefore have both George and George-TEST installed on the same machine. They will not share any state or data-files, and can be installed, upgraded, used, and uninstalled independently.

Legacy versions
